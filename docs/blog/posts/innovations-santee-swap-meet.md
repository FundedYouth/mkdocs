# Innovation at Santee Swapmeet

![](https://fundedyouth.org/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/2024-04-08_SparkInnovation_FeatureImage.png){width="500", loading-lazy}


In a bid to ignite the flames of innovation and creativity among the younger generation, FundedYouth and RCLasercraft have teamed up to orchestrate an exciting series of weekend STEAM events at the bustling Santee Swap meet. With a focus on Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics (STEAM), <!-- more --> these events promise to captivate the minds of young enthusiasts and foster a spirit of ingenuity that transcends traditional boundaries.

The collaborative efforts of FundedYouth and RCLasercraft aim to do more than just provide a platform for learning – they strive to inspire the next generation of innovators and problem-solvers. By instilling a love for STEAM subjects at an early age, these events empower youth with invaluable skills that will serve them well in an increasingly technology-driven world. So, if you’re looking for an opportunity to engage your child’s mind and bond as a family, head down to the Santee Swap meet on Sundays and join the movement towards a brighter, more innovative future.

Every Sunday, families flock to the Santee Swapmeet, but now there’s an extra reason to visit – the opportunity for children and teenagers to immerse themselves in the dynamic world of 3D printing and design. Through interactive workshops and lessons led by experts in the field, participants are not only taught the fundamentals of 3D printing but also encouraged to unleash their imagination and turn their ideas into tangible creations.
